SynaptikOne Open-Source Projects

SynaptikOne explores the boundaries between science, computing, and mathematics to develop free, bio-inspired, and innovative tools. Discover our research projects, open to contribution and accessible to all.

GENOME_COMPRESSOR

GENOME_COMPRESSOR is a bio-inspired data compressor based on DNA encoding. This open-source project reinvents algorithmic compression schemes by drawing inspiration from natural mechanisms.

View the project on GitHub | Download the PDF guide on Zenodo

SECURE EDITION (prototype in progress)

Experimental encryption module for GENOME_COMPRESSOR, intended for sensitive contexts. A professional version may be offered later under specific conditions.
