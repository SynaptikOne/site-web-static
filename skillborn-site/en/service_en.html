SynaptikOne Services & Expertise

SynaptikOne provides nature-inspired solutions in data compression and cybersecurity. We also offer scientific and technical support for open-source projects and collaboration with laboratories, researchers, and engineers.

Bio-Inspired Compression

Research and experimentation on algorithms inspired by living systems and genetic principles. Adaptable to academic or exploratory needs.

DNA Data Protection

Development of hybrid security modules (classical and bio-inspired cryptography) to protect genomic and biomedical data streams.

Technical Support

Assistance in integrating GENOME_COMPRESSOR into existing processing pipelines and support for laboratories, researchers, or engineers.

Knowledge Sharing

Organization of workshops, seminars, and training on bio-inspired compression, DNA encoding, and open-source approaches in computational science.
