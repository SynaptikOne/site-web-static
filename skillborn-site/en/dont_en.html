Support GENOME_COMPRESSOR

GENOME_COMPRESSOR is a bio-inspired, open-source, and scalable data compressor developed to explore the intersections between biology, computing, and security.

The project is structured around several modules:

  • Free Edition: fully open source, released under the MIT License (Python)
  • Experimental Modules: integration of hybrid encryption mechanisms (Rust)
  • Advanced Extensions: applied research in post-quantum cryptography (Rust)

Why donate?
