Privacy Policy

At SynaptikOne, we respect your privacy. This page explains what data is collected, for what purpose, and how it is protected.

Data Collected

  • Email address (via the contact form or newsletter subscription)
  • Anonymous browsing data (statistics via Google Analytics)

Use of Data

Your email address is used only for:

  • Responding to your messages
  • Sending the newsletter if you are subscribed

Data Retention

Data is stored securely, without sharing or selling to third parties.

Your Rights

Under applicable data protection laws, including GDPR, you have the right at any time to:

  • Access your data
  • Correct or delete your data

To exercise your rights, contact us at: contactsynaptikone@gmail.com
